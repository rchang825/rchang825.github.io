About Me

Hi, I'm Ruby Chang, a fullstack software engineer based in Seattle. I love problem-solving in a way that makes a difference, no matter how small. This can be in the form of implementing a technical solution to a problem myself, or serving as a rubber duck in a debugging session with a teammate to help them through a challenging issue.

My most recent experience was as a student in the Software Engineering Immersive program at Hack Reactor. I spent 1000+ hours pair programming on algorithmic code challenges and building fullstack web applications individually and as a team. It was extremely rigorous but SO MUCH FUN.

When I'm not in front of screen coding, I may still be in front of a screen gaming or reading on my Kindle with my cats stepping on my keyboard every 30 minutes to remind me to blink. I also love to cook, bake, and crochet.
